## Updated tutorial flow

The tutorial now separates the sample-document workflow into two steps:

1. Download the Sample PDF with the `Sample PDF` button.
2. Upload the downloaded ABF Annual Report 2025 PDF through the `Upload PDF` component.

The upload step explains that the simulated pipeline starts automatically only for the supported sample document. Other PDFs are rejected by the prototype validation modal so that the displayed prompt-chain outputs and Human Review examples remain aligned with the ABF Annual Report 2025 sample data.
